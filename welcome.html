How to add a domain to Spam Detector:
Press the ALT key on the keyboard to bring up the menu bar, click [Tools] -> [Message Filters]
Find Spam Detector and double click it, or click it once, then click Edit. Under "Match any of the following" click any of the [+] buttons and change the 1st dropdown to "From", and the 2nd dropdown to "Ends With". Then type the domain into the text fiel and press [OK] 
How to change theme of client:
Click on the hamburger menu (3 lines at top right of app, then goto addons and Themes.
Then when the Add on manager opens, click the Themes button on the left.
I've allready added a lode of themes to the client you can choose from on that list but if you want somthing other than whats on there, go back to Recomendations (2 buttons above Themes), scroll down, click Find more add-on, then click on the themes button at the top.
